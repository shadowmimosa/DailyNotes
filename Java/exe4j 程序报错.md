## exe4j 报错

> No JVM could be found on your system. Please define EXE4J_JAVA_HOME to point to an installed 64-bit

需要配置环境变量 `EXE4J_JAVA_HOME`
变量值为 jre 路径
eg.
`EXE4J_JAVA_HOME` - `C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.8.0_241\jre`
